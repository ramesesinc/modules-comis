COMIS (Cemetery Operations and Management Information System)

* Cemetery Resource Management 
    - Manage Cemeteries, sections, blocks and resources e.g. Niche, Tomb, Mausoluem and Ossuary 
    - Tracking, monitoring and allocation of resources

* Burial Permit Application
    - Application for New, Renewal and Cancellation of burial permit application 
    - Automated computation of fees, surcharges and penalties based on cemetery resources 

* Online Transactions Support 
    - Online renewal of burial permit application 
    - Online payment of application 

* Work Order Management 
    - Scheduling, tracking and monitoring of work orders such as resource maintenance
    - Effective allocation of cemetery personnels

* Records Management 
    - Archiving of historical burial records and applications 
    - Efficient search and query facilities on burial records and applications
